RiftStats is a competitive Riftbound resource built by someone who has been playing card games professionally since before most of the current player base knew what a mana curve was. The matchup chart reflects expert judgment from high-level Bo3 play — not blended aggregate data from the full player pool. Guides are written to make you better, not to make you feel good about where you already are.
